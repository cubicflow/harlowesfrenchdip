Historical restoration

Old school vibe

Nestled on historic Union Street, 43. E Union started life with a warm full wood interior kissed with hints of 1930’s art deco. When Harlowe's took over the space in 2016 it began a full restoration of the original dining room. Every wooden panel was carefully removed and painstakingly restored to prestige. Step inside and let the charming atmosphere transport you back in time.
