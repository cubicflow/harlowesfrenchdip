"La Fin Du" Mustard

Mustard made from beer

Harlowe’s is one-upping the mustard game. Our house recipe uses La Fin Du Monde beer (direct from Chambly, Quebec) to form its distinct&nbps;taste.

We take our mustard, made from wholesome seed, cayanne, and a batch of house spices. Mixed and blended, we marinate the mustard base in La Fin Du Monde overnight which is then strained, chilled, and served fresh.

And what about the La Fin Du Monde beer itself? If you aren’t lucky enough to catch it on tap it’s also available in a bottle as part of our Craft Beer Program.
