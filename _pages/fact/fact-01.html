Who are we?

Eyes, meet stomach

Centered in historic Old Pasadena, CA, Harlowe's is a place where modern and vibrant ingredients play melodic renditions of classic dishes — an ensemble of signature sandwiches and upscale pub fare. Think Pulled Pork and Texas Toast across from fresh oysters, burgers and beer cheese... with beignets for dessert, of course. By using only the freshest product we aim to elevate treat each component with the utmost respect.
